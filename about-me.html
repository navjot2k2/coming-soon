About

A global perspective, built from the ground up.

Navjot Punni is the founder of SunRE Global, a business strategy and IT consulting practice built on 15+ years of hands-on experience across channel sales, international distribution, and global business development.

Having worked across South Asia, Africa, the APAC region, and North America — with organizations including TVS Motor Company, Oracle, and Softchoice — Navjot brings a rare combination of strategic thinking and commercial execution to every engagement. He understands what it takes to enter new markets, build high-performing teams, and drive sustainable growth across diverse geographies and industries.

At SunRE Global, Navjot partners with business leaders to cut through complexity and deliver clear, actionable strategies — whether that's entering a new market, transforming operations, or navigating technology decisions with confidence.
